Our Donate Plan

All donations to this fund are initially used for hunger relief and medical emergencies. With the help of local organizations, we try to use the best possible use of this fund. After this worst tragedy, we want to look out at their long-term rehabilitation.

Food for a family

In times of disaster, hunger is the main problem. Every time of a disaster, Our initial goal is no people can suffer from hunger. And every single person will be furthermore not worried about food.

Shelter for a family

In times of disaster people's one of the most wanted things is a shelter. A safe shelter gives a stress-free mind which is more important in this type of situation. Rehibalition's most effective procedure is to provide a safe shelter.

Education for a child

Education is one of the basic needs of every people. The children must need proper education for a bright future. During the time of the disaster, they become mentally unhealthy. The education sector, such as schools, colleges, and Madrasa, is where a child can get a healthy mentality.
